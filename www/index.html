How it works

Order from our website

Browse wide variety of grocery items on our website. Choose any grocery items that you like, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

Choose your delivery time

Once you paid for your order, you can choose when you want the order to be delivered to your home. In case you need to change the dates, just contact us and we can help you with that. Please note additional fee might apply.

Receive your order at your doorstep

Once you've confirm the date and time, just relax and wait from the comfort of your home. In the morning before the delivery we will send you a message to remind you about the delivery. We will send a second message once your order left our warehouse.
